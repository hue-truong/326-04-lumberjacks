About Us
At Lancer, we make dreams a reality.
Lancer's mission is to provide everyday freelancers and job-seekers with not-so-everyday oppurtunities, without the typical complexity of searching for those oppurtunities. Here, we provide you with potential offerings based on your interests, previous experience, and time spent on our site. This allows for the most personal and easy-to-navigate job application experience ever made.
Our team consists of four young, ambitious minds who are about to fully enter the world of job-searching, and we've strived to create the most convenient and accessible means of applying with the goal of making the experience as streamlined as possible.
So what are you waiting for? Go find your dream job.
